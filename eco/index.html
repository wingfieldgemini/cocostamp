Eco Statement

Biodegradable by nature.
Luxurious by design.

Each CocoStamp vessel is a fallen coconut given a second, ceremonial life — sourced from responsible farms, finished without synthetic coatings.

Our promise

From farm floor
to fine bar.

We source only mature shells that would otherwise return to the soil. No plantations cleared. No plastic laminates. No microplastics in your guest's last sip.

Every CocoStamp is finished with food-safe processes — water, ultraviolet light, and time. Nothing else touches the surface your guests will touch.

100%Biodegradable Shell
0Synthetic Coatings

Sourcing

Mature, fallen coconut shells purchased directly from cooperative farms in the Philippines and Sri Lanka. No clearing, no monocultures, no harvest pressure.

Finishing

Triple-washed in filtered water, UV-cured for sterility. Zero synthetic coatings, zero food-contact plastics, zero microplastics shed in service.

End-of-life

Fully compostable in 90 days under standard conditions. Engraving leaves no residue — your mark returns to the earth as naturally as the shell itself.
