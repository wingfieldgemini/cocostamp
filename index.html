Eco Statement

Biodegradable by nature. Luxurious by design.

Each CocoStamp vessel is a fallen coconut given a second, ceremonial life — sourced from responsible farms, finished without synthetic coatings, and fully biodegradable once its service is complete.

We source only mature shells that would otherwise return to the soil. No plantations cleared. No plastic laminates. No microplastics in your guest's last sip.
