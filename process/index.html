The Process

Three steps.
Zero compromise.

From concept to countertop in under three weeks, engineered to arrive exactly as we imagined it — and exactly as you approved it.

i.

Upload Your Logo

Share your mark in any vector format. Our atelier refines it for engraving — a one-time $50 Logo Master sets your file for life.

ii.

Precision Engrave

Ultra-fine lasers score each shell by hand-supervised batch. Approve the digital mock-up before a single coconut is touched.

iii.

Global Delivery

Triple-washed, UV-sanitized, straw-drilled and palletized. Expedited to any port of call — from Mykonos to Mustique.

In Detail

What happens between
upload and arrival.

01

Atelier file preparation

Your vector mark is reviewed by our engraving lead, refined for tonal contrast on coconut shell, and saved as your Logo Master — invoiced once at $50.

  • SVG, AI, EPS, or PDF accepted
  • Curve smoothing & line-weight tuning for laser fidelity
  • Digital proof returned within 48 hours
02

Hand-supervised laser engraving

Each shell is mounted, oriented, and engraved on calibrated CO₂ lasers. A studio technician inspects every batch under raking light before approval.

  • Sub-millimetre fidelity on serifs, crests, and ornament
  • Engraving depth tuned per shell density
  • Reject rate < 2% — discards composted on-site
03

Sanitization & finishing

Three water rinses, one UV pass, one quality inspection. Each piece is pre-drilled to 8 mm for eco straws and seated in linen-wrapped cases.

  • Food-grade triple wash + UV treatment
  • Diamond-cut flat base (zero-spill verified)
  • Linen-wrapped cases of 25
04

Global, palletized dispatch

Air or sea — your choice. Dispatched from Lisbon, Singapore, or Miami. Tracked door-to-door, with a dedicated handler on Estate-tier orders.

  • Boutique & Club: 10–14 days
  • Estate: scheduled drops on annual calendar
  • Carbon-offset freight as standard
